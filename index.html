FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What services does Venkatesh Hospital provide? ▼
We offer comprehensive multispeciality services including Cardiology, Orthopaedics, Nephrology, Urology, Neurosurgery, Paediatrics, Obstetrics & Gynaecology, Pulmonary Medicine, Dentistry, General Medicine, General Surgery, Laparoscopic Surgery, and Homeopathy. We also have 24/7 ICU and Trauma Center facilities.
Do you provide emergency services? ▼
Yes, we provide 24/7 emergency care with a fully equipped Trauma Center and ICU. Our emergency team is always on standby. Call us at +91 90280 80694 or +91 90280 80695 for immediate assistance.
How can I book an appointment? ▼
You can book an appointment through our website's booking form, by calling us at +91 90280 80694, or via WhatsApp at the same number. Our team will confirm your appointment within 30 minutes.
Are specialist doctors available every day? ▼
Most specialists have set visiting hours on specific days. Our general medicine, ICU specialists, and emergency team are available 24/7. Please call us to check specific doctor availability and schedule accordingly.
Do you accept insurance? ▼
We accept most major health insurance plans and government health schemes. Please contact our billing department with your insurance details for pre-authorization before your visit.
What ICU facilities are available? ▼
Our ICU is equipped with advanced ventilators, multi-parameter monitors, bedside ultrasound, and other critical care equipment. We have IDCCM-certified intensivists managing the ICU round the clock.
Is laparoscopic surgery available? ▼
Yes, Dr. Amol Mulay (MBBS, DNB Surgery, FMAS) performs general and advanced laparoscopic surgeries including cholecystectomy, appendectomy, hernia repair, and many other minimally invasive procedures.
